NewTek TriCaster TC1 and NDI® Devices Demo

DigiTek Systems will demonstrate the TriCaster TC1 4K video switcher and streaming solution, along with NewTek’s LiveGraphics software. In addition to Matt Drabick from DigiTek Systems, Alex Udell, NewTek sales rep for the US Southeast region, will be present to help answer any technical or pricing questions. We plan to have a variety of NDI® products at the demo working with the TriCaster TC1 including NewTek NDI® PTZ camera and PTZOptics camera, the NewTek Connect Spark SDI, BirdDog Studio NDI®, and more.
